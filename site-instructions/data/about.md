## Short Curriculum Vitae

- **Since 2024** — Assistant professor at Ecole des Ponts ParisTech, researcher at CERMICS.
- **2022–2023** — Courant Instructor and Flatiron Fellow, mostly working with [Joan Bruna](https://cims.nyu.edu/~bruna/), Professor at the Courant Institute and the Center for Data Science, New York University.
- **2020–2022** — Postdoc in machine learning under the supervision of [Nicolas Flammarion](https://www.di.ens.fr/~flammarion/) in the Theory of Machine Learning team of EPFL.
- **2017–2020** — Ph.D in machine learning under the supervision of [Francis Bach](http://www.di.ens.fr/~fbach/) in the [SIERRA team](http://www.di.ens.fr/sierra/).
- **2017** — Graduated from [École des Ponts ParisTech](http://www.enpc.fr/) (Marne-la-Vallée). [Master on national policies for sustainable development](http://www.enpc.fr/mastere-specialise-papdd-politiques-actions-publiques-developpement-durable).

Master thesis in stochastic processes, PDEs and interacting particles system at [Cermics](https://cermics-lab.enpc.fr/), with [Tony Lelièvre](http://cermics.enpc.fr/~lelievre/) and [Julien Reygner](https://cermics.enpc.fr/~reygnerj/).

Master thesis in statistical physics at [Collège de France](https://www.college-de-france.fr/site/antoine-georges/index.htm) (Paris), with [Antoine Georges](https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antoine_Georges) and [Michel Ferrero](https://www.cpht.polytechnique.fr/cpht/correl/people/ferrero/homepage.htm).
- **2007–2012** — [Lycée Louis-Le-Grand](http://louislegrand.org/) (Paris, France).

For my master thesis in theoretical physics, I had the chance to win [Le Rivot Prize](http://www.academie-sciences.fr/fr/Laureats/laureats-2015-prix-thematiques.html) from the [French Academy of Sciences](http://www.academie-sciences.fr/fr/).
