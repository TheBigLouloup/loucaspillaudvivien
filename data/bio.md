I am an assistant professor at Ecole des Ponts ParisTech and a researcher at CERMICS, in the Applied Probability team. Prior to this, I was a Courant Instructor/Flatiron fellow both at the Courant Institute of NYU and the Flatiron Institute, where I worked mainly with [Joan Bruna](https://cims.nyu.edu/~bruna/). Before, I was a postdoc in the Theory of Machine Learning group of [Nicolas Flammarion](https://www.di.ens.fr/~flammarion/) at EPFL. I did my Ph.D. in the [SIERRA Team](https://www.di.ens.fr/sierra/), under the supervision of [Francis Bach](http://www.di.ens.fr/~fbach/) and [Alessandro Rudi](https://www.di.ens.fr/~rudi/) on stochastic approximation for high-dimensional learning problems.

I am the recipient of an ANR JCJC grant (2026) for the project **GALA** (Geometric perspectives on the Analysis of Learning Algorithms, [summary](Articles/GALA_summary.pdf)) and of a CNRS PEPS JCJC grant (2025). If you are a prospective PhD student or postdoc interested in these topics, feel free to reach out by email.
